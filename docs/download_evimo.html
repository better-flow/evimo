DAVIS 346C

Sensor:

  • Resolution: 346 x 260
  • Datasheet: download

FOV: 70°

  • Intrinsics calibration file: download

Recordings:

  • Total recording time ~30 minutes
  • Up to 3 independently moving objects objects
  • Multiple types of scenes (varying backgrounds and motion speeds)
